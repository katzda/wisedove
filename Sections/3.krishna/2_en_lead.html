2 Corinthians 11:14 And no wonder, for even Satan disguises himself as an angel of light.

Do you agree that in this world, there is a physical death and evil? This physical world is a manifestation of God, who/what He is/wants. The very first commandment of God to humans was: "You must not eat fruit from the tree that is in the middle of the garden, and you must not touch it, or you will die." Because all humans are not perfect, all "fell short of the glory of God", we die. But through a sacrificial love, there is a certain everlasting life. The word "sacrifice" is crucial, mentioned 318x in Christian Standard Bible (CSB translation).
